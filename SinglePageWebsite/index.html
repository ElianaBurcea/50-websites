Envoy Passport

A better way to sign in.

Envoy Passport saves you from typing your details every time you visit an office that uses Envoy. And your phone never even has to leave your pocket.


Offices around the world have ditched their paper and pen, and use Envoy for a better visitor sign-in experience.


And now, there’s an even better way: Envoy Passport uses iBeacon technology to send your sign-in details directly to the iPad, so you can skip the typing. It’s sign-in at it’s fastest, so you can get on with your day.

How it works


Download


Download Envoy Passport, and sign up with your name and basic information. Then enable Bluetooth and permissions.


Visit


Head to any office that uses Envoy.


Tap


The Envoy iPad will detect your Passport using Bluetooth. When your avatar pops up on the screen, just tap it to sign in! Your name and details will appear automatically.

Collect stamps in your Passport

Get a new stamp every time you sign in with Passport. You’ll even see stamps from any prior office visits!

Where can I use Passport?

Passport will work at any location that uses Envoy. Note that if Bluetooth is disabled on a company’s iPad, you’ll be unable to use Passport while the “Envoy Passport is offline” notice is on the iPad screen.

How does the iPad detect Passport?

Envoy iPads recognize Passport using Apple’s iBeacon technology. Since Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is the underlying communication method for iBeacons, you’ll need to enable Bluetooth on your iPhone or Android phone.


Who can use Passport?

Anyone with an iPhone or Android phone can use Passport.

What if I don’t enable Bluetooth?

Without Bluetooth, the Beacons will not “see” you, so you will not be able to use Passport.
